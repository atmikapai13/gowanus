Definitions:
Properties – Assessed commercial properties (residential/nonprofit/govt typically exempt)

Assessment – Mandatory fee on property owners, collected by DoF with property tax, remitted to BID twice yearly. BID defines its operating budget and the formula(s) used to calculate each property owner's assessment.

Budget – Total annual operating funds (assessment ~78% + fundraising, contracts, grants, and program fees.)
